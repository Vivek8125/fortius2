About Fortius Minechem

Building Excellence in Minerals Since 2011

Established in 2011, Fortius Minechem has emerged as a trusted name in the mineral industry, specializing in Calcite Powder and Dolomite. With a strong commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction, we have grown into a leading manufacturer and supplier serving various industries, including paints, plastics, ceramics, construction, and agriculture.

Our Commitment to Quality

At Fortius Minechem, we prioritize high-purity minerals, advanced processing techniques, and stringent quality control measures to ensure that our products meet the highest industry standards. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility is equipped with modern technology to deliver consistent and premium-grade materials to our clients.

Our Founder - Bharat Patel

Fortius Minechem was founded by Bharat Patel, a visionary entrepreneur with a deep understanding of the mineral processing industry. With years of experience and expertise, he played a key role in shaping the company's growth. Before establishing Fortius Minechem, he also served as a Director at Deca Microns, further strengthening his legacy in the field of industrial minerals.
